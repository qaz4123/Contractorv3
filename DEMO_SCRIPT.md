### Part 2: Lead Intelligence - The "WOW" Factor (5 min)

**What to Show:**

1. **Quick Lead Input**
   - Type a property address (e.g., "123 Main St, San Francisco, CA 94102")
   - Google Maps autocomplete kicks in
   - Hit Enter to create lead

2. **AI Analysis Happens Automatically**
   - System searches property data
   - AI generates comprehensive intelligence
   - Lead score calculated (0-100)
   - Takes ~5-10 seconds

3. **View Lead Detail**
   - Navigate to the newly created lead
   - Show the intelligence tabs:
     - **Property Intel**: Year built, square feet, bedrooms, bathrooms, estimated value
     - **Owner Intel**: Owner name, ownership type, motivation factors
     - **Financial Intel**: Estimated equity, liens, tax status
     - **Renovation Opportunities**: Specific areas with ROI estimates
     - **Sales Approach**: AI-recommended strategy

**Key Points:**
- "This is the magic - we turn a simple address into actionable intelligence"
- Lead Score tells you if it's worth pursuing
- Renovation Opportunities show exactly what to pitch
- Sales Approach gives your team a ready-made strategy

**Demo Tips:**
- Use a real address for best results
- Point out the renovation opportunities with ROI
- Emphasize how this saves hours of research

## üìù Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Where does the property data come from?**
A: We use a combination of public records, property databases, and AI analysis of web searches.

**Q: How accurate is the lead scoring?**
A: The AI considers 20+ factors including property condition, owner motivation, equity, and market trends. It's been validated against actual conversion data.

**Q: Can I customize the renovation recommendations?**
A: Yes! The system learns from your feedback and specialties.

A: No! It works for residential, commercial, and renovation contractors.
